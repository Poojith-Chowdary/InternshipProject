About Us

The Multi Banking System Interface is targeted to the future banking solution for the users who have multiple bank accounts in multiple banks. This interface integrates all existing banks and provides business solutions for both retail and corporate.

This system acts as a standard interface between the clients and all the banks. By using this portal, any client who maintains accounts in various banks can directly log on to the Multi-Banking System Interface and make any kind of transactions. In the backend, the system will take care of the entire obligation required to carry on transactions smoothly.
